Hawaiian Food

Hawaiian food is a unique blend of different influences from all around like Polynesian,asian,japan,china,etc... A couple main ingredients hawaii is known to eat is fresh grown fruit,seafood,shrimps, and seaweed. Poi is a traditional hawaiian staple made from taro root. The taro is made by either cooked,pounded, and then mixed with water to create a smooth paste. It is often served as a side dish. There are other food like Kalua Pig which is a classic Hawaiian dish made by slow roasting a whole pig in an underground oven called an imu. This isn't consider a dish its more of a sweet treat, shaved ice is a beloved hawaiian treat.
